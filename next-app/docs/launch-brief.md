# Governance Framework Launch Brief

## Initial Capability Focus

- Incentive Alignment
  - Level 2 Success Criteria: Documented mapping between AI system objectives and organizational KPIs, reviewed by at least two independent stakeholder groups.
  - Level 3 Success Criteria: Incentive structures adjusted and validated through outcome monitoring across one full scoring cycle (≥90% of assessed roles show alignment between incentives and governance effectiveness metrics; ≤10% regression in post‑change audits over two quarters).

- Measurement Infrastructure
  - Level 2 Success Criteria: Established baseline metrics for three key safety dimensions (robustness, alignment, compliance) with automated reporting for 80% of pilot systems.
  - Level 3 Success Criteria: Metrics integrated into executive dashboard with trend analysis and early‑warning alerts (≥95% automation coverage for pilots, <48h mean time to signal on threshold breach, ≥2 quarters of trend completeness).

- Authority Mapping
  - Level 2 Success Criteria: RACI structure defined for all Gate 1–2 decisions, endorsed by executive sponsor.
  - Level 3 Success Criteria: Authority maps actively used in two oversight processes, with feedback loop documented and applied (≥40% reduction in escalation time for safety‑critical decisions; ≥80% adherence to mapped routes in audit samples).

## Pilot Assignments

- Incentive Alignment → Product Development
  - Risk: Misalignment between system and business outcomes
  - Mitigation: Quarterly cross‑review with Strategy; align pilot KPIs with product OKRs
- Measurement Infrastructure → AI Operations/Engineering
  - Risk: Metric definition churn and instrumentation gaps
  - Mitigation: Establish an MDM for metrics; freeze v1 metric catalogue for two quarters
- Authority Mapping → Risk & Compliance
  - Risk: Resistance to authority redistribution during incidents
  - Mitigation: Executive sponsor endorsement; decisive‑mode playbook training

## High-Level Timeline

- Month 0–3: Capability matrix scoring, gap analysis, ownership assignment.
- Month 3–6: First scoring cadence cycle, initial remediation playcards executed.
- Month 6: Publish baseline maturity scoring and quick‑win results.
- Month 6–9: Deliver meta‑dependency map and starter dashboard prototype.
- Month 9–12: Second scoring cycle with live dashboard reporting to executives.
