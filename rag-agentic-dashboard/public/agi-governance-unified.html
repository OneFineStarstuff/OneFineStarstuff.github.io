Every AI component designed with governance interfaces from inception. No post-hoc governance bolting. All SDLC gates include governance checkpoint.

Resonant Alignment
Continuous alignment via feedback loops between human intent, model behaviour, and governance policy. Not one-time RLHF — continuous calibration.

Graceful Degradation
Governance failures trigger proportional capability reduction, not total system failure. Sentinel kill-switch reduces to safe mode, not shutdown.

All AI decisions accompanied by causal reasoning chains. Provenance tracking from data source through inference to output. Audit-ready at all times.

No single entity (human or AI) has unchecked authority above defined capability thresholds. Multi-party authorisation for Stage 6+ systems.
