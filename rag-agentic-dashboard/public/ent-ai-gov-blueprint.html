Why now
Between 2024 and 2026 the global AI regulatory perimeter hardened: EU AI Act entered staggered enforcement (GPAI Aug 2025, high-risk Aug 2026), NIS2 transposed, DORA took effect for FSI (Jan 2025), NIST AI RMF 1.0 + GenAI Profile became a de-facto US baseline, and ISO/IEC 42001:2023 matured into an auditable AI Management System standard. Simultaneously, agentic AI moved from pilot to production, creating new risk classes — prompt injection, tool-mediated exfiltration, supply-chain model poisoning — that legacy GRC tooling cannot observe.
Board decisions requested (within 90 days)
  • Adopt this blueprint as the enterprise standard
  • Appoint a Chief AI Officer (CAIO) with veto authority over non-compliant deployments
  • Ring-fence an investment envelope of 0.8–1.4% of IT OPEX for 2026–2027
  • Adopt the quarterly AI Governance Scorecard review
