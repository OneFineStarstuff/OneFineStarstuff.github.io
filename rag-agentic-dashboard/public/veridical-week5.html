Analytical Rationale & Calibration Framework

Week 5 is the programme’s inflection point from infrastructure buildout to optimisation engineering. Three critical state transitions: (1) Embedding abstraction layer deployed — VR-001 transformed from MEDIUM to LOW. The enterprise can now hot-swap between OpenAI, Cohere embed-v3, and self-hosted e5-mistral-7b with zero downtime and <2% accuracy variance. Shadow index (15% of corpus) validated in production failover. (2) Offline reranker evaluation produced decisive results: Cohere Rerank v3 +4.1 pp (p<0.001, n=2,400); ensemble approach +4.6 pp — only +0.5 pp above single-model, confirming single-vendor is sufficient. CTO approved shortlist Mar 10 — Week 6 sprint unlocked. (3) Accuracy at 88.2% (+0.8 pp WoW) confirms diminishing pre-reranker returns. The reranker integration projects the programme’s single largest accuracy jump: +3.5–4.5 pp, potentially achieving the 92% North Star at Week 7 rather than Week 10.

Finance department onboarded as the fourth pilot group (361 total users, +44% above target). Retrieval accuracy at 88.2%; with reranker integration in Week 6, the 92% North Star is now projected achievable at Week 7 — three weeks ahead of the Week 10 gate. Budget: $532K of $1.42M (CPI 1.11, $140K projected underrun).
